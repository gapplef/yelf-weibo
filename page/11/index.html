Stray Birds 291

Posted on 2015-04-21 In Poem , StrayBirds Views:

Clouds come floating into my life from other days no longer to shed rain or usher storm but to give colour to my sunset sky.
— Rabindranath Tagore

从往昔飘入我生命的云朵
已不会再带来雨水或风暴
只会为我落日的天空增添色彩
- Don't Panic!

评：当你老去，再回首，往昔的风雨艰难只会让你的人生更丰富多彩
